\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

Every GRPO reward formulation we tested fails to calibrate sycophancy: $\dsyc$-based rewards collapse because the direction conflates warranted revision with capitulation, and $\rthree$-only GRPO fails as NLI rewards likewise lack evidential warrant.
The probe reward trap causes behavioral death 1{,}300 steps before entropy collapse, evading standard monitoring.
Dense per-token supervision succeeds where per-completion RL fails~(\S\ref{sec:discussion}), identifying supervision granularity as one plausible factor.
